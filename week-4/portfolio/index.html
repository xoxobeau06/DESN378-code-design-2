About Me!

Hi, I'm Sydney, but you can call me Syd!

I am a design student here at Eastern, and I have no clue what I'm doing! I love everything soft feminine and pink, and if I can be like a cozy grandma I want to be! My goal is to continue studying design and hopefully full-stack web development. If I'm not drowning in homework you can usually find me at my job at LUSH downtown, reading a good book, playing animal crossing, or hanging out with my dog (as seen in the picture) and boyfriend! As scary and as difficult as it may seem right now I hope to continue growing as a designer and developer!
